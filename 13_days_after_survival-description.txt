It's been 13 days since hordes of zombies invaded your city and killed most of the people. But you are one of the last survivors. Try to stay alive as long as possible and shoot the bloodthirsty attackers with various guns. They will first walk towards you but be aware, soon enough they will pick up their speed and come running to get you, so you better knock them out with a head shot.
This game is as creepy as it gets and you will experience sheer horror. Upgrade you weapons, health and others to perform even better and have those evil creatures not stand a chance against you. Are you ready to face these scary zombies?
