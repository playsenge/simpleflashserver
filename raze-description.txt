Raze is the ultimate futuristic space shooting game. If you like it, check out Raze 2 and Raze 3 which are sequels to this awesome title. Before you start fighting, you can customize your character and choose their playing style and appearance - there is over 20 different outfits to choose from. Once you have created your character you can enter one of the different game modes such as campaign and deathmatch. The gameplay is fun and features various different elements such as double jumping and jump boosts. You can fight with a variety of different weapons and interact with objects on the levels. The campaign mode allows you to work your way through various missions and follow an interesting storyline. This game has great playability and the graphics and level design are fantastic. Join the fight today and conquer Raze!
