Absolutely new part of the extreme adventures Moto X3M 5 will take you to the Pool Party with unforgettable rides, jumps and obstacles. Fearless biker must overcome updated 22 levels, which will be held against the backdrop of the beach, sea, sun and palm trees.
The rules remain the same: get to the finish in the minimum amount of time. Perform complex stunts and rotate in flight to get minus 0.5 seconds at the end of the race. A few successful jumps will allow you to earn 3 stars and set your own time record in the Pool Party.
