Plazma Burst is the first installment of the Plazma Burst series, released by Eric Gurt on March 24, 2009. The game is set on a nearby planet, where Humans, Grubs, Falkoks, and Drones fight the player and each other. The player takes control of the Marine, the main protagonist, and fights their way through each level. The player is given the opportunity to upgrade their weapons and armor between each level in the 'between-time shop' to take on more advanced and stronger opponents and survive.
