Trollface is back again with new adventures! Get ready for some winter sports with a difference in Trollface Quest 4! Funny sequel to the weird puzzle game series for the troll in everybody. Solve all riddles and you will see at the end of the day it's all about trolling. Just click on the objects in the picture and see what's going to happen to the funny troll.
Be creative and don't try to find any signs of logic in Trollface Quest 4. The solutions are often more confusing than the riddle itself to try to think outside the box and give every crazy idea a chance. This funny Trollface is completely unpredictable and you will definitely be entertained for a while. Can you become the new olympic champion?
